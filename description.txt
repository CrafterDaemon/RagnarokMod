Ragnarok. A great calamity in norse mythology, Is now a mod that intends to allow Thorium and Calamity to be used together with minimal issues.
Adds more content for Thorium classes, Merges Thrower + Rogue, and creates a more coherent experience throughout all stages of the game.

UPDATE 1.5.2.1
Ragnarok: The April Chaos Update

────────────────────────────────────────

BALANCE

  - Decreased the rarity of the Voidseer Pearl from Light Red to Pink
  - Increased the sell price of the Voidseer Pearl to 50 gold
  - Charred Idol is now crafted with a Void Heart rather than the Voidseer Pearl
  - The Eldritch Soul Artifact now requires an Eldritch Shell Fragment to craft
  - Reduced the max Bloodlust of the Bloodflare armor sets to 100
  - Added the Calamity life steal cooldown to the Bloodflare Grim Visage set bonus' life steal
  - Bloodlust from the Bloodflare armor sets now resets when a boss is spawned
  - Readjusted many of the numbers on the Nightfallen armor set
  - The Nightfallen armor set is now a Corrupted Healer armor set
  - Increased Scoria Dualscythe damage (80 → 100)
  - Auric Greatscythe: Increased damage (450 → 600), reduced right-click damage multiplier (x20 → x10), the multiplier no longer applies to the right-click swing, right-click now has longer iframes to prevent hitting the same enemy twice, right-click bolt now correctly aims toward the cursor
  - Moved Unbreakable Combat Ukulele to pre-Devourer of Gods
  - Ectambourine no longer animates on right-click

RECIPES

  - Auric Greatscythe: Marble Scythe + The Black Scythe + 5 Auric Bars

BUG FIXES

  - Fixed a bug that caused the Leviathan Heart to not always be correctly classed as a Healer item
  - Fixed the Leviathan Heart tooltip incorrectly listing the amount of attack speed it provides
  - Fixed a bug that caused the set bonus for Tarragon helmets to not load properly
  - Removed the random squares from Irradiant Infinity projectiles, Red now renders properly

APRIL CHAOS

  - There is now a 1/5,000 chance for ||any sound to be replaced with the Primordial Wyrm spawn sound|| on April 1st only.

  - Added the April Chaos config, featuring the following toggleable options:
      - Obnoxious Bard: Riffs play a random track (37 secret riffs!)
      - Summelee: Melee and Summoner damage classes are merged
            Summoner class was still hungry for content, so we fixed that.
            By giving all of Melee to Summoner.
            Your minions now respect you. Your sword does not.
      - Radiagic: Healer and Mage damage classes are merged
            Healer just felt like an extension of Mage, so we fixed that.
            By replacing Mage with Healer.
            All magic is healing if you believe hard enough. Or hit hard enough.
      - Roganger: Rogue and Ranger damage classes are merged
            Rogue is taking back its roots.
            By absorbing Ranger.
            They were always the same person. One just had better shoes.

NEW CONTENT

New Title Screen
Ragnarok now has its own title screen!
Thanks to Kyou for the epic title screen art!

New Bard Mechanic: Riff
Using a Riff weapon will initiate a Riff, granting bonuses or effects to all nearby players
Riff Volume and Riff Screen Effects are controllable client-side via config
There are 6 Riff weapons in this update.
They are listed below (spoilers)
  - Scourge's Frets - Dropped by Desert Scourge
  - Infected Ocarina - Dropped by Hive Mind
  - Shredder - Dropped by Perforator Hive
  - Stellar Riff - Dropped by Astrum Aureus
  - Devourer of Sines - Crafted Post-DoG
  - Dragon Force - Dropped from Yharon
