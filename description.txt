Ragnarok. A great calamity in norse mythology, Is now a mod that allows Thorium and Calamity to be used together with minimal issues. 
Adds more content for Thorium classes, Merges Thrower + Rogue, and creates a more coherent experience throughout all stages of the game.

[h2]Boss Rework Information:[/h2]

[h3]Grand Thunder Bird[/h3]
Rev:
Moves and attacks faster and unlocks additional attacks upon reaching 50% health instead of 33% health
Spawns more ThunderZaps from above
Moves faster
Charges faster
ThunderZaps fly faster

Death:
Charges twice and faster
Moves even faster
ThunderZaps fly even faster
Spawns 1 additional Storm Hatchling
Shoots even more ThunderZaps

Bossrush:
Moves and charges way faster
Shoots even more ThunderZaps
StormHatchlings also move way faster
ThunderZaps fly even faster

[h3]Queen Jellyfish[/h3]
Rev:
BubbleBomb no longer requires the player to be on the ground
Slightly increased movement speed
Shoots BubbleBombs more frequently in all phases, BubbleBombs fly a little bit faster

Death:
Moves significantly faster
Shoots BubbleBombs even more frequently, BubbleBombs fly significantly faster
Increased  defense while casting whirlpool
Increased summon cap for every summon from 3 to 4

Bossrush:
Queen Jellyfish and her summons move and shoot much faster
Whirlpool  has way stronger tracking
Increased the defense while casting whirlpool even more
BubbleBombs flies way faster
