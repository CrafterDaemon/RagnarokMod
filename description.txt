Ragnarok. A great calamity in norse mythology, Is now a mod that intends to allow Thorium and Calamity to be used together with minimal issues.
Adds more content for Thorium classes, Merges Thrower + Rogue, and creates a more coherent experience throughout all stages of the game.

Ragnarok: Riffs & Recalibration

NEW CONTENT

New Title Screen
Ragnarok now has its own title screen!
Thanks to Kyou for the epic title screen art!

New Bard Mechanic: Riff
Using a Riff weapon will initiate a Riff, granting bonuses or effects to all nearby players
Riff Volume and Riff Screen Effects are controllable client-side via config
There are 6 Riff weapons in this update.
They are listed below (spoilers)
  - Scourge's Frets - Dropped by Desert Scourge
  - Infected Ocarina - Dropped by Hive Mind
  - Shredder - Dropped by Perforator Hive
  - Stellar Riff - Dropped by Astrum Aureus
  - Devourer of Sines - Crafted Post-DoG
  - Dragon Force - Dropped from Yharon

Localization

Bug Fixes
  - Nightfallen Armor now has a proper Set Bonus

  - Profaned Scythe now inflicts the correct debuff
  - Lich Phylactery now properly accepts 3x Essence of Havoc

Balancing

  - auto-reuse on right click enabled

Calamity Bell and Lamentation buffed:
  - Now function like SCals Brimstone Fire and Gigablasts

Astral Ripper buffed:
  - Now spawns falling stars twice as often

Radio Mic overhauled:

  - Center projectile strikes target with shadowy tendrils

Fractal
